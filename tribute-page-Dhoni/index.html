MS DHONI

Early Life

Mahi Born on July 07, 1981, in Jharkhand, India, Mahendra Singh Dhoni started taking his interest in Cricket and other sports from a very early age. MS Dhoni always struggled with his studies. He completed his education at DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir, Jharkhand. In this institute, he was not only a great cricketer but also excelled in badminton and football.Dhoni was such a great player that he was selected in teams of all of these sports at a district level.

Dhoni is from a typical family. His father’s name was Pan Singh, who worked in MECON. MECON stands for Metallurgical & Engineering Consultants Limited. Devki Devi is his mother’s name. This great cricketer also has an elder brother and a sister. His brother’s name is Narendra Singh Dhoni and he is a politician. His sister is Jayanti Gupta, who is a school teacher.Dhoni married Sakshi Singh Rawat on 4 July 2010. This girl was his schoolmate in DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir. Dhoni became the father of a baby girl in 2015.

World Cups Winning

MS Dhoni is most successful and famous Indian captain .Under his captaincy Indian Cricket Team achieved so many trophies and series.He is the only captain in the world to won all trophies of ICC.He won three major trophies ,first he won ICC world T20 in 2007.Then under his captaincy India was able to won 50 over World Cup in 2011.After this Indian Cricket Team won the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.

In 2007 ICC decides to arranges an event World T20 .This was a new idea and also some sort of interesting one .No one was knows how to tackle situations in T20 as it was new for everyone.At that moment Indian Cricket Board (BCCI) decides to send a team of young players and to handle command of team to MS Dhoni first time.The rest is became history. In this world cup Indian Captain handled the situation so calmly and the right way that Indian Team able to won the Title .Then afterwards the magic of MS Dhoni was at peak,So boards decides to hand over the full time captaincy to him.

2011,the year of expectation as the world cup was held in IThe 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup was the tenth Cricket World Cup,And the golden words of Ravi Shastri “"Dhoni finishes off in style.......A magnificent shot into the crowd.India lifts the world cup after 28 years,party starts in the dressing room and it's Indian Captain who has been absolutely magnificent at the night of final."

In 2013 Indian Cricket Team was going from transition phase many young players were in team. Again MS Dhoni uses his all experience and got best from his team and clinch the victory.The Golden Bat was won by Shikhar Dhawan and Golden Ball was won by Sir Jadeja .By winning this Champions trophy MS Dhoni became first ever and only captain in the world to won all ICC Trophies.
